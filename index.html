Gameplay

PokerHouse is easily one of the most entertaining and exciting games around. Our strategy is to create a sophisticated environment for wacky game play so that players are chalenged like never before. Because when the moves are unpredictable, players are encouraged to make bolder and un-characteristic decisions. This builds up an atmosphere of intrigue, thrill and surprises --- a stark contrast to typical poker games led by calculated moves based on the earnest study of opponents *yawn*. At PokerHouse, players will never know what comes next. Now, that’s a real test of skills!

When players are in the house, they will have to put their money where their mouth is...or not. We give players the power to decide that by deploying our unique array of game mechanics. Pokerhouse turns the game on its head so that the highs and lows of every bet pushes players to keep trying to best their opponents the next round.

So forget studying the table, cards, faces, body language, and whatever it is you do to make the most of each play. Poker should be fun. And fun is fun is fun. When the stakes are blind and players are crazy, that’s when the fun gets real in PokerHouse.

The question is, “Do you dare?”
